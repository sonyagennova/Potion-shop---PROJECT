Potion Shop

Welcome to the Potion Shop! Press the "Open shop to customers" button to start the simulation.

Your task is to implement the handleData function in the script to manage the shop's inventory and orders based on incoming data.

You should reject orders if there are not enough ingredients in stock to fulfill them, and reject pickups if the customer has not paid enough.

Afterwards, there are a few different directions you could take this project, try to implement some of the following features:

  • Track the money that the shop has earned from sales, and the money that has been spent on restocking ingredients. Try to judge the shop's profitability over time.
  • Keep a track of past orders and their outcomes, in case the wizard taxman comes by.
  • Implement a way to display the current inventory of ingredients, and the active orders in the ledger section below.
  • Use cookies to persist the shop's state money and inventory data across sessions. (You may also need to do so for the internal data in the head script)
  • Play into the fantasy - add more functionality you might think a potion shop would need!
You don't have to implement all of these features, but the more the store feels like a real potion shop, the better!
